Angel Acosta
It was an honor marching BK, a summer I will never forget! Marching with the Blue Knights gave me life skills such as a great work ethic, determination, and taught me to believe in myself. As a result, I am now a part of the “Commandant’s Own, U.S. Marine Drum &Bugle Corps”.

Angel Acosta, Blue Knights Drum and Bugle Corps Member, ‘09
Becky Paschke

Assistant Principal and Activities Director
Conifer High School, Conifer, CO
